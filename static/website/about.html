A little info about MyHustle

  • Who are we?

    Monday, 20 June 2022 myhustlezw

    MyHustle is the trading name for HustleRoutine Pvt Ltd, a software development company based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. HustleRoutine was established in 2021. Our goal is to build scalable reliable software which solves real world challenges.

    HustleRoutine website

  • Our revenue model.

    Monday, 20 June 2022 myhustlezw

    Our revenue model is simple and summarised below.

    Revenue Model Amount
    Subscriptions, Pay per view, Support(Tips) 30cents for every dollar earned
    Identity verification One time fee of ZWL equivalent of USD $1.00

    We do not believe in advertising supported revenue, and unlike our competitors we do not aim to become a middleman.
    Our goal is to provide a seemless experience for our clients, and to provide a platform for them to monetize without worrying about the processes around tech, payments and the economy.

  • Payments process.

    Monday, 20 June 2022 myhustlezw

    Creators have to request a withdrawal from their dashboard, afterwhich we process and approve the withdrawal to their nominated bank accont or mobile money wallet. Should any issues arise during this process our staff will reach out via phone or email.
